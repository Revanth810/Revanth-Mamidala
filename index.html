Krishna Teja Chitty

 

Education

I'm a Doctoral (PhD) student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, working under the supervision of Dr. Arun Somani in Dependable Computing and Networking Laboratory. I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from University College of Engineering, Osmania University, Hyderabad, India in Summer 2017.

Research

My research interests are Machine Learning and Parallel Computer Architecture/Computing. I primarily work at the intersecion of systems and Deep Learning which include designing compressed Neural Network algorithms for efficient inference on hardware accelerators (TPU-like) and general purpose processing devices (CPU, GPU). I'm also working on different problems (fault tolerance, memory accesses) with respect to special purpose DNN hardware.
